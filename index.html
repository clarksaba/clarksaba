Clark Saba's HCDE 533 Digital Fabrication Page

Hi, I'm Clark.

I'm a UX designer attending my final quarter here with HCDE.
I'm excited to learn entirely new skills in bridging the digital and the physical; and I'm curious to
understand the many ways digital fabrication might be useful to interaction design.











Assignment 1: Press Fit Construction Kit

Constructing curves by press-fitting pieces of laser cut pentagons and hexagons.

Assignment 2: Parametric Modeling with Grasshopper

Creating parametric models for a construction kit that takes inputs for different depth sizes.

Assignment 3: Getting Started with 3D Printing

Setting up my 3D printer, experimenting with settings and printing nested objects.

Assignment 4: Subtractive Manufacturing

Constructing a box made up of flat panels with sides that snap to each other using boxjoints and dogbones.

Assignment 5: Getting Meshy, and Lamps

Practicing with meshes, and fabricating a lamp.

Assignment 6: Molding and Casting

Fabricating molds with 3D printing, silicone and hydrostone.

Assignment 7: Final Project Proposal

Planning how to combine multiple digital fabrication techniques to create a custom chess set.
